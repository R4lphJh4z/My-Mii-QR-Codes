Steps to Scan QR Codes in Tomodachi Life
Mii Maker:
 1. Go to Mii Maker
 2. Tap QR Code/Image Options
 3. Go to Scan QR Code
 4. Scan It!
 5. Exit Mii Maker
 6. Go To Tomodachi Life
 7. Now, Go to Town Hall
 8. Tap Create Mii
 9. Tap Transfer From Mii Maker
 10. Now, Tap the Mii that you scan

And now thats it!
